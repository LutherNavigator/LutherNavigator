About Luther Navigator

Social media, User-generated content, Travel tips-style application

Luther Navigator is a web-based application designed to allow the Luther College community to connect and share information from their experiences around the world.

After creating an account, users are able to:

  • Post reviews of hotels, restaurants, and other attractions from wherever their journeys take them.
  • Search for specific Luther study away programs to share in the experiences and insights of others who have done the program or will do it in the future.

Luther Navigator was launched in May 2021 and was designed by three Luther computer science students as a part of their senior project. The students—Will Allen ’21, Nell Himlie ’21, and David Oniani ’21—worked in collaboration with staff in the Luther Center for Global Learning to launch this new travel tool.
